## Academic Research & Publications

**"AI Systems and User Perception: A Case Study of the Real-time Face Recognition Filter <Ectoplasm>"**
- Original Korean Title: *인공지능 시스템의 경계 설계와 사용자 수용성: 안면 인식 필터 <Ectoplasm> 사례 분석*
- Submitted to: Korean Digital Content Society Summer Conference 2026
- Keywords: Computer Vision, Facial Recognition, AI Hallucination, Politics of AI, User Experience
- Focus: Media-philosophical analysis of algorithmic hallucination, boundary design in AI systems, and user complicity in surveillance technology through the lens of 19th-century séance practices (drawing on Sconce, Steyerl, and Crawford)
